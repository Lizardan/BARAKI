Privacy Policy

Game: BARAKI
Developer: Unio Games / Lizardan
Last updated: 16 July 2026

1. What we collect

When you play BARAKI we may process:

  • Unity Authentication player ID (anonymous or linked sign-in)
  • Display name and profile fields you choose to save (nickname, rank/points stubs)
  • Friends list and presence status while the hub or game is running
  • Match / lobby session data needed to connect you to other players
  • Technical diagnostics required to operate multiplayer services

2. How we use data

Data is used only to operate the game: authentication, multiplayer lobbies, friends/presence, cloud-saved profile, and game updates. We do not sell personal data.

3. Service providers

We use Unity Gaming Services (Unity Technologies) for authentication, lobby, relay, friends, and cloud save. Processing by Unity is subject to Unity’s privacy documentation.

4. Retention

Account and profile data are kept while your player account exists or until you request deletion. Match session data is temporary.

5. Your rights

You may request correction or deletion of profile data tied to your player ID by opening an issue on the project repository:

https://github.com/Lizardan/BARAKI/issues

6. Children

BARAKI is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us via the repository issues link above.
